Biryani

Description

Biryani is a popular rice dish made with aromatic Indian spices either with vegetables or egss or meat.

First, the meat (or eggs or vegetables) and the rice are cooked seperately. Then, they are arranged in layers and cooked together to infuse the rice with all the aromatic flavours./p>

Ingredients

    COOKING THE RICE
  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 4 to 5 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
    CHICKEN MARINATION
  • 400 grams Chicken Thighs
  • 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Juice of lemon
    TO MAKE BIRYANI
  • 2 Onions, chopped lengthwise
  • 5 to 6 Garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 inch Ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 to 3 Green chillies, slit
  • 2 Tomatoes , diced
  • 6 to 8 strands of Coriander leaves or cilantaro
  • 1/4 cup Mint leaves, loosely packed
  • Oil
  • Cinamon stick
  • Cardamom pods
  • Cloves
  • Bay leaf
  • Cumin seeds
  • Biryani or Tandoori Masala
  • Butter or Ghee
  • Water

Directions

    COOKING RICE

  1. Wash and soak the rice in water for 15 minutes.
  2. In the pot, add rice along with water and salt and bring it to boil on a high flame.
  3. Reduce the flame to medium and cook till the rice is cooked through 80%
  4. Drain and keep this aside.
    CHICKEN MARINATION
  1. In a bowl, add the chicken , turmeric powder , chilli powder , salt and lemon juice. Toss and keep this aside for 15 minutes.
    Making of Chicken Biryani
  1. In a pan , add oil
  2. Once oil is hot, add Cinamon sticks, cardamom pods, cloves, bay leaf, cumin seeds and saute for a minute.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook for three minutes.
  4. After 3 minutes, add garlic , ginger and green chillies.
  5. Flip the chicken and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Add Onion , tomato coriander leaves, tandoori masala and give it a good mix.
  7. Cook it for a minute and then spread it evenly to cover the base of the pan.
  8. Add partially cooked rice, spread evenly and garnish with some more mint and coriander leaves.
  9. Finally, add butter and sprinkle some more tandoori masala powder and splash a 1/4 cup of water.
  10. Cover this and cook on low flame for 20 minutes or until rice is cooked.
