Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken! The ultimate comfort food your entire family will love! Make the best flavour-packed, creamy and delicious authentic Indian Butter Chicken at home with this easy recipe.

Serve it with Butter naan, Roti, Chapati, Plain Paratha, Steamed fluffy Basmati Rice or Jeera rice for a simple Indian meal. In this post I share the recipe to make the authentic North Indian Butter Chicken known as Murgh Makhani.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon finely minced or grated (or use paste)
  • 2-3 cloves finely minced or crushed
  • 1 ½ pounds about 2-3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into ¾-inch chunks
  • 4 tablespoons or 8 oz can of tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon

  • 1 teaspoon Fenugreek I use powder, but seeds or mustard seeds can be used too, optional*

  • 1 cup sub for half & half or yogurt for low fat
  • Hot cooked rice and naan for serving
  • 1 medium onion diced

Directions

  1. Heat a large skillet or medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Add the oil, butter, and onions and cook onions down until lightly golden, about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add ginger and garlic and let cook for 30 seconds, stirring so it doesn’t burn.
  3. Add the chicken, tomato paste, and spices. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until everything is cooked through.
  4. Add the heavy cream and simmer for 8-10 minutes stirring occasionally. Serve over Basmati rice or with naan.
