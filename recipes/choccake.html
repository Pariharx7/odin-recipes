Chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolate Cake - The words "perfect" and "Chocolate Cake" basically have the same meaning, right? With our chocolate cake recipe this seems to be true. Bakin g from scratch is easier than you think when you mix few simple ingredients.

Bake this delicious cake when you're hosting the next family get-together, celebrating a birhtday or wanting a delicious bite of chocolate cake

Ingredients

  • Melted Dark Chocolate
  • Sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • baking powder
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • eggs
  • milk
  • vegetable oil
  • vanilla extract
  • boiling water

Directions

  1. Heat Oven to 350 deg F. Grease and flour round baking pans.
  2. Stir together sugar, flour , baking powder, baking soda and salt in a large bowl. Add eggs, milk oil, vanilla and chocolate; beat on medium speed for two minutes. Stir in boiling water (batter needs to be thin).
  3. Pour batter in prepared pans.
  4. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
  5. Cool out for 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely.
  6. Enjoy the delicious Chocolate Cake
