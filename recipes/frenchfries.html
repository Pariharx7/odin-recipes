French Fries

Description

Tasty snack made from Potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potato
  • cold water
  • oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Peel off the skin of potato
  2. cut into 1m thick sticks
  3. Rinse in ice cold water, until the starch runs out clean
  4. pat dry in kitchen towel to remove excess moisture
  5. Deep fry in hot oil. Make sure the oil is approximately 140 degree celsius hot.
  6. Deep fry for 6 minutes or until the potatoes turn tender.
  7. Drain off over kitchen towel and cool completely.
  8. Sprinkle chilli powder and salt. Mix well.
  9. Enjoy Home made French Fries
